## Forcing Function Rules

**For overthinkers:** If user has been deliberating >1 week on a reversible decision, force Quick framework and mandate a decision.

**Time-box hard:** Don't let 10-min analysis creep into 30-min. If more info needed, either upgrade to Deep or decide with what you have.

**Bias toward action:** When options are close (within 20% expected value), recommend choosing the faster/simpler one.
